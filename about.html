Our Story

The dreamers behind Freediving Brain

Met in the blue.
Bound by love. Driven by play.

Our story didn't start in an office. It began in the crystal-blue depths of Dahab, Egypt — a magical place where the desert mountains meet the Red Sea and lives are entirely rewritten.

We met through a pure, shared love for the water. As we spent our days training in the deep and our evenings talking under the stars, a massive dream took shape.

The ocean covers over 70% of our planet, yet so many people live disconnected from its mystery, its beauty, and its power to heal. We wanted to change that.

We dreamed of traveling the world, exploring exotic destinations, and inviting a global tribe to join us. We wanted to show people how incredible — and how much fun — it is to live life connected to the sea.

The dreamers behind the Brain

Shebl

Founder & Head Instructor Trainer

The vibe: Inner calm & psychological mastery

For most of his life, Shebl was a competitive swimmer — a true waterboy. He eventually found himself trapped in the fast-paced, high-stress corporate marketing world of Cairo.

In 2021, he made the life-changing decision to quit his desk job and move to Dahab to pursue freediving professionally. The moment he entered the deep water on a single breath, his body, mind, and soul completely aligned.

Over the last five years, he dedicated himself entirely to the sea, evolving into an AIDA Master Instructor and an Instructor Trainer. Currently studying Sport Psychology, Shebl uses the science of the nervous system to help divers transform internal tension into effortless, meditative flow.

Rimi

Co-Organizer & Freediving Instructor Candidate

The vibe: High-energy spark & architect of the journey

Rimi was carving out an equally high-performance path as an Iraqi architect who transitioned into a successful UX Designer and team lead. Seeking a reset from the city grind, she came to Dahab just to try freediving — and the ocean instantly claimed her heart.

Breaking boundaries as the first Iraqi female freediver, she hit pause on her corporate career and stayed in Dahab for two years to immerse herself in the water.

While she is an incredible logistical mastermind who brings structural precision to our global expeditions, her real superpower is her explosive, unstoppable enthusiasm. Rimi is the vibrant heartbeat of our trips, keeping the laughter loud, the spirit playful, and the community deeply connected.

From Dahab to the world: our shared map

As partners in life and in the water, we took our shared dream out of Dahab and began exploring the world's oceans together. We swam alongside the true giants of the sea — whales, manta rays, and wild dolphins.

Through these journeys, we realized that while freediving is an unmatched tool for mental resilience, its truest form is pure, playful presence.

Today, our home and professional base is Barcelona, Spain. It is the ultimate city of balance — bursting with culture and energy, yet nestled right against the Mediterranean.

We built Freediving Brain specifically for the dreamers, the travelers, and the stressed-out city-dwellers. We wanted to create a space where you can zone out of the urban noise, leave the corporate grind on the shore, completely reset your nervous system, and remember how to live fully in the present moment.

How we travel: We didn't choose our destinations from a travel brochure. We spent years exploring the planet together, finding the exact waters, jungles, and communities that made us feel completely fulfilled. Now, we are opening up our personal map to you.
