AIDA 1, Introduction to Freediving

Pool only · 1 day · No experience required

€120

The gateway into freediving, and into a different relationship with your own mind. Designed for complete beginners. No swimming ability requirements beyond basic comfort in water. By the end of this day, you will hold your breath longer than you believed possible, not because we taught you a trick, but because we showed you what was always there.

1 full day, theory and pool
Small group, personal attention throughout
Indoor pool, Barcelona
AIDA 1 certificate included
All equipment provided
English instruction

What you learn

  • The Mammalian Dive Response, your biology's built-in reset
  • Breathwork and pre-dive relaxation protocol
  • Safe static apnea in the pool
  • Buddy system and safety fundamentals
  • Why the mind is the limit, and how to change that

AIDA 2, Freediver

Pool + Mediterranean · 2.5 days · No prior cert needed

€300

Your first full certification. Your first time in the Mediterranean. Your first time watching the surface disappear above you and discovering that the silence below is not frightening, it's the quietest your mind has ever been. AIDA 2 is where the identity shift happens. You become a freediver.

2.5 days, pool and open water
Maximum 6 students
Mediterranean open water dives
AIDA 2 certificate, 20m depth

What you learn

  • Static and dynamic apnea pool disciplines
  • Constant weight freediving to 20m
  • Equalization technique and freefall
  • Rescue breathing and buddy procedures
  • The mental game at depth, first contact
Reserve AIDA 2, €300

Advanced

AIDA 3, Advanced Freediver

Pool + Mediterranean · 3 days · AIDA 2 certified

€360

This is where the mental game becomes the dominant variable. As you push toward 30m and feel true freefall for the first time, technique is solid and equipment works, but the mind still wants to interrupt. AIDA 3 is where the Freediving Brain methodology delivers its most visible results. Students don't just reach depth. They reach it with calm.

3 days, intensive
Maximum 4 students
20–30m depth · first freefall
AIDA 3 certificate

What you learn

  • CO₂ & O₂ tables · static 2:45 & dynamic 55m
  • Constant weight to 20–30m with freefall
  • Mouthfill equalization introduction
  • Complete Freediving Brain mental protocol system
  • Nervous system regulation at depth
Reserve AIDA 3, €360
4
Performance

AIDA 4, Master Freediver

Pool + Mediterranean · 4 days · AIDA 3 + valid EFR certification

€480

Elite level. Small cohort. Deeply personal. At AIDA 4, every dive is a conversation between your nervous system and the ocean. You master mouthfill equalization, FRC dives, and lung packing, the technical apex of recreational freediving. The prerequisites are real: AIDA 3 and a valid EFR certification. Not everyone arrives here. Those who do leave changed.

4 days, intensive personal coaching
Maximum 3 students
38m depth · 3:30 static · 75m dynamic
AIDA 4 certificate

What you learn

  • Full mouthfill equalization mastery
  • FRC dives and lung packing techniques
  • Deep rescue and emergency protocols
  • Decompression and narcosis awareness
  • Personalised depth and performance coaching
Reserve AIDA 4, €480

Professional

AIDA Instructor Course

10–21 days · Max 3 candidates · STA 4–5min · CWT/FIM 40–50m

€1,200

Taught personally by Shebl, AIDA Instructor Trainer. The Instructor Course assesses rather than trains, you arrive already meeting the performance standards, and we shape you into a teacher: the mental methodology, the nervous-system protocols, and the philosophy that changes how students relate to depth. It begins with a mandatory pre-course assessment and runs 10 to 21 days. Maximum three candidates. This is not a production line. It is a transmission.

10–21 days, flexible, assessment-based

  • Freediving Brain mental performance methodology
