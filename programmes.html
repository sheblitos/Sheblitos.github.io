How a day works

Dry on the mat. Calm in the water. Closer by the end.

01

Dry — breathe & let go

We start on the mat, fully dry. Simple breathing, relaxation, and a first taste of real calm. No water, no pressure — just the team learning the same tools side by side.

02

Pool — trust & presence

Into the shallow pool as buddies. You hold a relaxed breath, stay calm, and look after each other — the buddy system that quietly builds real trust. Most people surprise themselves on the first try.

03

Open water — the shared adventure

For groups who want the full experience, we head to open water: a calm, fully guided dive together, and the kind of shared moment a team talks about for years.
